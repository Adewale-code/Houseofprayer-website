Welcome to RCCG House of Prayer Parish!

At RCCG House of Prayer Parish, we are a vibant and spirit-filled church where lives is transformed through prayer, worship,
and the word of God. Located in the heart of our community, we are a parish of the Redeemed Christain Church of God(RCCG),
a global ministry committed to sharing the love of Chist and making a lasting impact on lives woldwide.



Church Breif History

House of Prayer Parish was establshed 10th of July 2010 as a Parish under the Redeemed Christain Church of God, church started holding
her meetings at a flat in Abesan estate, ipaja Lagos state(Members apartment), stayed there for about 11 months(July 2010-July 2011)
later secured a permanent site(11th August 2011). Located at "House 3, 4th Avenue, Peace estate,baruwa, Lagos state."

The church was still an open ground, it wasn't the modern type, we were still worshipping on bare floor and we managed ourselves for another 4-5 years. The members started supporting, the first family in house of prayer were the Chukwu's family., joined us in 2011 and they're still members till date.
There were many storms and challenges but God has helped his church thus far, there was a time the shed we used for the church at the time fell on the ministers, But thank God it didn't lead to death, when it happened the church was challenged again and we had to start worshipping under the tree.

House of Prayer Parish is now under development, it's now an Area Parish.
Area 017, Region 20, Lagos Povince 15 Apapa family.
